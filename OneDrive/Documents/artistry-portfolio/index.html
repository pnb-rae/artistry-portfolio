About World Art Day

What is World Art Day?
World Art Day is celebrated internationally to promote awareness of creative activity worldwide. It's a recognition of the influence of art in human culture and expression.

When is it celebrated?
Every year on April 15, the birthday of Leonardo da Vinci — one of the most iconic figures in art history.

Why is it celebrated?
To acknowledge the role of art in fostering creativity, innovation, and dialogue among people of all backgrounds.

Benefits of World Art Day
- Encourages cultural exchange
- Supports artists and communities
- Inspires young creators
- Promotes peace and understanding through creative expression

How can it be improved and promoted?
- Hosting public exhibitions and workshops
- Leveraging social media campaigns
- Integrating art into education
- Supporting local artists and art fairs
